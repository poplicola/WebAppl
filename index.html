Made by a Polymath

Founded in 2008, Polymath Workshop is a small team of web developers and designers whose passion is crafting software and websites. Located just outside Chicago, we build appliations for both local and international clients of large-to-medium sized businesses.

Polymath's principal, Jay Margalus also develops games at Lunar Giant Studios, co-founded hackerspace Workshop 88, chairs the Chicago chapter of the International Game Developer's Association, and speaks occasionally around Chicago about game and web design.
